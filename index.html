📘 उपयोग गाइड (Hindi Guide)

Burnout Risk Analyzer ek self-assessment tool hai jo aapko yeh samajhne mein madad karta hai ki aap mानसिक aur physical burnout ke kitne kareeb hain.

यह कैसे काम करता है?

  • Pehle apni basic working details (hours, sleep, chhutti) bharein.
  • Fir 18 sawalon ka jawab dein — har sawal 0 se 4 ke scale par hoga.
  • Tool teen scientific dimensions par score calculate karta hai: Emotional Exhaustion, Cynicism/Detachment, aur Personal Accomplishment.
  • Final mein aapko ek overall risk score (0-100) aur risk level milega — Low, Moderate, High ya Severe.

Dimensions ka matlab

  • Emotional Exhaustion: Aap kitna thaka hua aur drained feel karte hain.
  • Cynicism/Detachment: Aap apne kaam ya clients se kitna distant ya negative feel karte hain.
  • Personal Accomplishment: Aap apne kaam mein kitna capable aur satisfied feel karte hain (kam score = zyada risk).

Recovery Plan kya deta hai?

Premium unlock karne par aapko ek 14-din ka structured plan milta hai jisme daily micro-actions, client boundary set karne ke scripts, aur weekly progress tracker shamil hain — khaas taur par freelancers aur CA professionals ke liye design kiya gaya.

Zaroori Note

Yeh tool sirf self-awareness ke liye hai, medical diagnosis nahi. Agar aapko lagataar severe symptoms ho rahe hain, toh kripya kisi qualified mental health professional se sampark karein.
